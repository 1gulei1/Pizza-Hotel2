Pizza "Mushroom" Recipe

Dough for mushroom pizza

  1. Flour - 3 tbsp.
  2. Dry active (quick-acting) yeast - 7 g
  3. Serum or water - about 1 tbsp.
  4. Olive oil - 2 tbsp. spoons
  5. Salt - 0.5 tsp.

Pizza topping

  1. Fresh mushrooms - 300 g
  2. Tomatoes - 1-2 pcs.
  3. Cucumbers (can be salted or pickled) - 1-2 pcs.
  4. Sweet pepper - 1-2 pcs.
  5. Hard cheese - 150-200 g
  6. Olives - 10 pcs.
  7. Vegetable oil
