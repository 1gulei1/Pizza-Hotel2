Pizza "Hawaiian" Recipe

Dough for Hawaiian pizza

  1. Flour - 200 g.
  2. Water - 130 ml.
  3. Dry yeast - 1 tsp.
  4. Vegetable oil - 1 tbsp.
  5. Sugar - 0.5 tsp.
  6. Salt - 0.5 tsp.

Pizza topping

  1. Pineapples - 120 g.
  2. Chicken fillet - 150 g.
  3. Mozzarella cheese - 100 g.
  4. Canned corn - 2 tbsp.
  5. Tomato sauce - 3 tbsp.
