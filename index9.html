Pizza Margherita "Recipe"

For the dough for 2 pizzas of 300 g each:

  1. 400 g of flour (+ for dusting)
  2. 200 ml of water
  3. 10 g of pressed yeast
  4. 1 Art. l. of olive oil
  5. 1/2 tsp. sugar
  6. pinch of salt

For the sauce:

  1. 150 g of pelati tomatoes
  2. 2 cloves of garlic
  3. 3 leaves of basil
  4. pinch of salt
  5. a pinch of sugar
  6. 1 Art. l. olive oil

For the filling:

  1. 150 g of mozzarella cheese
  2. 1 tomato
  3. 10 basil leaves (for serving on ready-made pizza)
